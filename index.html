Practice stock trading with no real money.

PaperStreet lets you buy and sell stocks using a fake cash balance so you can learn how the market works without any risk.

You start with $10,000 in paper money. Search for stocks, build a portfolio, and track how your picks do over time.

How it works

  1. Create a free account
  2. Get a $10,000 starting balance
  3. Search for stocks and buy shares
  4. Watch your portfolio grow (or not)
